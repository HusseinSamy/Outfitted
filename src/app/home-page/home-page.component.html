What is outfitted?

outfitted is not meant to be a start up at all, it's only an idea that I have decided to put the time to make it alive. it was a momentary idea that came into my mind while I was shopping for a new outfit and that question lighted my mind; why don't we make some kind of software that facilitates the frustration that comes with the outfit choosing?
